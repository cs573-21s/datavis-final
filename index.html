This visualization design represents asset types in the middle connected to pie charts of the farm's areas at the bottom and detailed asset list at the top. The asset types are categorized by color throughout. The circles are also encoded by size. When implemented with interaction, hovering over each circle will show all of its connections.
This Adobe Illustrator design is a radial planting timeline. Each line represents the planting time of a specific plant; yellow represents the growth period, and green represents the harvest period. Future work will be implementing an interactive visualization with a tooltip that shows plant details in the center.
This visualization is a Choropleth map of the farm showing the number of logs by area. Mouseover highlights the area and gives details about the type of logs. There is also zoom functionality for the map.
This visualization is a calendar heat map showing the number of farm logs per day from 2018 to 2020. The days are color-coded depending on the number of logs and and each day shows the exact number of logs by hovering over the day.
